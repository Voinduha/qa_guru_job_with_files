Pravda
Pravda is a general purpose blockchain with PoA consensus (PoW in future). It's a part of Expload - a platform for a games with distributed economy.

Network node is build on top of Tendermint, is written in Scala and runs on JVM.
We have our own virtual machine for "smart contracts" (not exactly) and translators from .NET (and EVM in future) bytecode.
